My Journey

My professional journey began in customer service, where I supported a UK-based sports and fashion group through multichannel platforms, including email, live chat, social media, and inbound/outbound calls. In this role, I achieved a near 85% CSAT score, consistently met KPI metrics, and gained hands-on experience with CRM tools and e-commerce operations, such as order processing and customer follow-up. I also volunteered to take on new responsibilities, such as being one of the first to handle inbound calls, which contributed to the project’s success and led to increased trust from the client.

After leaving customer service, I explored various career paths and discovered a growing interest in digital marketing and project management. I pursued the Google Digital Marketing & E-commerce Certification to build a strong foundation in key areas like SEO, PPC, social media, email marketing, performance marketing, e-commerce, and customer relationship management. Through this certification, I completed case studies and gained practical insights into data-driven strategies and campaign optimization.

Currently, I am working as an intern for an e-commerce clothing brand that is starting from the ground up. In this role, I am assisting with e-commerce and digital marketing efforts, with a particular focus on performance marketing. This hands-on experience is helping me hone my skills and apply what I’ve learned in a real-world setting.

Looking ahead, I am passionate about learning more and keeping a versatile skill set. I am particularly drawn to growing fields that embrace innovation and change, and I’m always open to new opportunities that allow me to grow and make a meaningful impact.
